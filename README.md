As part of my learning journey in Data Analytics, I had the opportunity to learn from **Satish Dhawale**, whose teaching helped me understand how to transform raw data into meaningful insights. I’m grateful for the knowledge and practical skills I gained through his guidance.

Recently, I built an **End-to-End Hospital Emergency Data Dashboard in Microsoft Excel**. This project helped me apply several important data analysis and dashboarding concepts.

🔍 **Key insights from the dashboard:**
• Total patients analyzed: **493**
• **Average waiting time:** 4.74 minutes
• **Patient satisfaction score:** 34.93
• **Gender distribution:** 248 Female | 245 Male
• **Admission status:** 51.52% Not Admitted | 48.48% Admitted
• **Most common age group visiting:** 51–79 years
• **Top department referrals:** General Medicine and Orthopedics
• **57% of patients were attended on time**, while 43% experienced delays.

🛠 **Skills and tools used:**
• Microsoft Excel

• Interactive Slicers
• Data Cleaning & Transformation
• Dashboard Design & Data Visualization
